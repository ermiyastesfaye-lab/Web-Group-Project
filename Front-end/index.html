Ethiopian Agricultural Products

Coffee

Ethiopia is renowned for its high-quality Arabica coffee. Grown in regions like Sidamo and Yirgacheffe, Ethiopian coffee is known for its distinctive flavors, often characterized by fruity and wine-like notes.

Teff

Teff is a small, ancient grain native to Ethiopia. It is a staple in Ethiopian cuisine and is the main ingredient in injera, a traditional flatbread. Teff is gluten-free and rich in nutrients.

Barley

Barley is another important cereal crop in Ethiopia. It is utilized in the production of injera, a fermented flatbread, and is also a key ingredient in traditional beverages like "t'ej" (honey wine).
